Happy Lunar New Year!!

The Chinese zodiac, or shengxiao (/shnng-sshyao/ ‘born resembling'), is a repeating cycle of 12 years, with each year being represented by an animal and its reputed attributes. In order, the 12 Chinese horoscope animals are: Rat, Ox, Tiger, Rabbit, Dragon, Snake, Horse, Goat, Monkey, Rooster, Dog, Pig. 2020 is a year of the Rat.

Rat

People who born on the year of Rat are:
smart,wealthy,successful and popular, rat are curious and thoughtful and like tidiness. But they can be stubborn and lack courage.

Ox

People who born on the year of Ox are:
Patient, cautious and industrious, oxes have strong staying power and don’t jump into making decisions. But they’re poor communicators

Tiger

People who born in the year of Tiger are:
Loyal, trustworthy and courageous, tigers are good at expressing themselves and like challenges. But they can be hasty and over-confident.

Rabbit

People who born in the year of Rabbit are:
Hope and peace, rabbits are compassionate and modest, with a good sense of humour. They’ll do anything to escape from a dull life.

Dragon

People who born in the year of Dragon are:
Ambitious, energetic and intellectual, dragons are romantic and strive for perfection. But they can be arrogant and impatient

Snake

People who born in the year of Snake are :
Wise, passionate, sympathetic and even-tempered, snakes are self-aware and good communicators. They can be fickle, jealous and headstrong.

Horse

People who born in the year of Horse are :
Positive, easygoing and warm-hearted, horses are dynamic generous and attract many friends. But they have a tendency to over-spend.

Goat

People who born in the year of Goat are :
Gentle, calm, kind, caring and polite, sheep are one of the most loved of the signs. Sometimes shy, sensitive and pessimistic, they have an artistic streak

Monkey

People who born in the year of Monkey are :
Self-assured, sociable and sporty, monkeys are versatile, flexible and creative. But they can also be jealous, suspicious and selfish.

Rooster

People who born in the year of Rooster are :
Punctual, honest, bright and warm-hearted, roosters are neat and like to keep busy. They can be critical, quick-tempered and hate dawdlers.

Dog

People who born in the year of Dog are :
Faithful, courageous and clever, dogs make great leaders and are good at keeping secrets. But they’re quick to find fault and can be distant.

Pig

People who born in the year of Pig are :
Chivalrous, optimistic and frank, pigs are kind and loyal friends and light of heart. Sometimes they can be a little too trusting.
